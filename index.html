About

I am a Taiwan-based linguist working within the generative framework. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Graduate Institute of Linguistics, National Tsing Hua University. I also teach undergraduate and postgraduate courses at National Chung Hsing University.

My research focuses on Vietnamese linguistics, with particular interests in syntax, semantics, and their interfaces.

Research Interests

  • Vietnamese linguistics
  • Generative syntax
  • Diachronic syntax
  • Syntax-semantics-pragmatics interfaces
  • Modal particles and sentence-final particles
  • Non-canonical interrogatives
  • Negation and scalarity

Current Projects

  • Non-canonical wh-interrogatives in Vietnamese
  • Modal uses of tense markers
  • Comparative syntax (Vietnamese & Chinese)
